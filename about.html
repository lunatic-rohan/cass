About Us

Computer Application Student Society (CASS) is a society affiliated to the Department of Computer Applications, Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology established in 2014. The aim of the society is to promote students on all fronts of life. This also gives a platform where all students of the department work together irrespective of their course and year of enrollment. The united efforts of the students bring vibrancy in their life and help them to understand their excellent potential on all fronts including academics. The members of this society work with full enthusiasm to organize various co-curricular activities such as sports, culture, technical etc, at regular intervals. The society is running under the leadership of Dr. Ratika Pradhan, Head Department of Computer Applications. The society is composed of the president, faculty coordinators and council members and student members who work together to bring the best outcomes from the various activities that CASS conducts. All the students of the Computer Application Department are default student members of the society. Hence, they are encouraged to plan a co-curricular activity with the consent of council members and faculty co-ordinators of the society.
