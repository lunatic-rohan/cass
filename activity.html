Our Activities

International Conference on Computing & Communication

International Conference on Computing and Communication (IC3) was initiated in the year of 2015 to provide a global forum for knowledge sharing for the budding as well as established researchers from the domains of Computing and Communication. The first ever IC3 was conducted in the month of March 2016. The reponse was encouraging a group of dedicated researchers have participated with full enthusiasm. Proceedings of the conference was published by Foundation of Computer Science, USA and made available on-line through their journal IJCA. The second IC3 was organized during March 2018 with more spirit and enthusiasm. This time was more encouraging having associated with esteemed publishing house Springer as publication partner. All the papers were published by Springer in a volume - Advances in Machine Learning and Data Analytics and indexed in SCOPUS.
Department of Computer Applications, SMIT proudly presents the IC3 in association with CASS

Basic Programming Workshop At Various Schools Of Nepal

With a vision of inducing general programming skills at basic level, the CASS conducted 3 days of programming workshop in 5 different schools of Sindhuli district in central Nepal. Around 300 students from classes 9-12 had participated for the workshop. Each session lasted 3-4 hours a day and all together 15 sessions were conducted for 5 different schools. These sessions helped in igniting the young minds with programming concepts and providing ample awareness regarding the correct utilizement of internet for smart studies. The major topics focused in the workshop were:-
● Q-BASIC programming.
● C programming
And basic knowledge on recent trends in the field of computer application like Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things(IOT),etc. The sessions were conducted both theoretically as well as practically in the computer labs of the respective school. Students were also handed with a lot of educational materials in the form of pdfs, ppts, etc. They were also made aware about the academics in SMIT and how SMIT has been tirelessly thriving for production of excellent professionals with the holistic approach on quality education and appropriate mentorship, which in turn encouraged many of the students to join SMIT for further studies.

Workshop In Arunachal Pradesh

CASS conducted a grand workshop in Pasighat, Arunachal Pradesh. A lot of young aspirants had participated in the workshop. Scholarship Scheme was also included in the workshop and was provided to the student having wonderful performance.

World Environment day

On the auspicious occasion of world environment day, the Department of Computer Applications in association with CASS conducted a Tree plantation drive.

Blood Donation Camp

A blood donation camp was organized by the Computer Applications Student Society, department of Computer Applications, SMIT at the Health Care Centre of the Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology (SMIT). More than 90 enthusiastic participants including employees and Students participated in the noble cause and received an overwhelming response. The students supported the medical teams to make the blood donation camp a major success. A team of doctors from CRH i.e; Dr. Nagendra Nath Bhuyian, Dr Amlan Gupta and Dr Rachna Lamichaney came for the noble cause from the Central Referral Hospital. The Central Referral Hospital, medical team and CASS distributed beverages and fruits to the donors. The medical team appreciated the Students for organizing a successful blood donation camp.
Speaking about the initiative,Dr. Ratika Pradhan, Head of Department, Computer Application, & President of CASS SMIT said, "We at SMIT are committed to provide improved health care facilities to our communities. We are proud to be associated with this well-intentioned cause of blood donation.By organizing such blood donation camps every donor is contributing towards the well-being of the society at large. We take this opportunity to thank the donors for their participation. We also appreciate the support provided by Central Referral Hospital."

Ethical Hacking Workshop

Two days workshop on Ethical Hacking and Cyber Security was conducted from 14th - 15th October 2017 by Computer Applications Student Society (CASS), Department of Computer Applications, Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology, Majhitar, East Sikkim in collaboration with IIT Hyderabad and Azure Skynet Pvt. Ltd. 150+ Students from various institutions from Sikkim, West Bengal, and Assam participated in the workshop.
Mr Manish Bhardwaj, well known cyber security expert delivered his talk covering various aspects of cyber security and ethical hacking. At the end of the session 10 participants were selected as finalists for the BrainTech Championship 2018 to be conducted later this financial year. Dr Amik Garg, Director, Sikkim Manipal Institute of Technology graced the occasion of opening session as chief guest.
